## Key Features Your App Needs to Compete

To stand out against the likes of Uber or a Lyft clone app, your software needs to be more than just a copy; it needs to be a refined tool.

- **Real-Time Geofencing**  
  Don't just track cars. Use geofencing to set specific boundaries for different pricing zones or service areas.

- **Smart Dispatch Algorithm**  
  Your taxi booking script should prioritize drivers not just by distance, but by their *heading* and historical performance to reduce ETAs.

- **Multi-Payment Integration**  
  Beyond credit cards, your taxi booking app must support local digital wallets, Apple Pay, and even *pay-by-link* to cater to diverse demographics.

- **Safety Toolkit**  
  In 2025, features like an SOS button, live trip sharing, and "hidden" phone numbers are no longer optional — they are the standard.
